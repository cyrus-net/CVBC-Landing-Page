FAITH AND COMMUNITY

Join us in worship and
fellowship

Christ Victory Baptist Church, founded in 1951 in Lagos,
NG, serves a vibrant faith-based community. We invite
you to experience our regular worship services,
engaging Bible study groups, youth programs, and
impactful community outreach initiatives. Our mission
is to spread the message of Christ while providing a
welcoming space for spiritual growth and fellowship.
Join us every Sunday at 9 am for Sunday School, 10 am
for our main service, and Wednesdays at 6 pm for Mid-
week Worship. Connect with us online on Facebook,
YouTube, and WhatsApp!
